About Me

I'm a robotics PhD student at Georgia Tech. I explore multimodal learning, vision-language models, and how AI can better perceive and interact with the real world.

Researches

  • Speculative Decoding for LLMs – improving LLM decoding efficiency via branch divergence tracking.
  • Vision-Language Pose Estimation – multi-modal pose models with SMPL synthetic supervision.
  • CAD Self-Repair – fixing failed CAD sequences using LLMs with geometry consistency constraints.
